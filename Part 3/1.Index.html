ICTM 2020

“Mental Health and Well
Being in the 4th
Industrial Revolution”.

ICT4M 2020 is a conference that gathers academicians, researchers, practitioners,
professionals, and students to address the challenges of Information and
Communication Technology (ICT) for the Muslim world. This conference has been
conducted seven times so far with the most recent location in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia on
23-25 March 2020. In conjunction with the World Congress on Integration and
Islamicization, ICT4M 2020 will be held on 27-29 March 2020 in Kuantan, Pahang. The
theme for this year is “Mental Health and Well Being in the 4th Industrial Revolution”.
All accepted papers will be submitted for indexing in IEEE Xplore. Selected papers will be
invited for publications in Scopus journals
